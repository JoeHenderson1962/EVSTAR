Is there a limit on the number of devices you can cover?

 

No, there is no limit on the number of devices you can cover, both new and existing as long as they fall within the product categories listed above.

 

Is there a limit on  the number of claims I can make?

 

You may file an unlimited number of claims in a 12-month period. There are annual dollar limits on claims, the maximum amount for households is $5,000 per household and $2,000 per single claim.  

 

What is covered?

 

Eligible products are covered for mechanical and electrical failures as a result of defects in the materials and workmanship, normal wear and tear, dust,  heat and humidity, power surge failures and Accidental Damage from Handling (ADH) like drops, spills and cracked screens on select portable electronics

 

Products eligible for ADH coverage are tablets, laptops, handheld gaming devices, and headsets. Tablets, laptops, and wearables are eligible for screen repair if the damage is the result of normal usage.

How do I file a claim?

 

You can file a claim online at www.techcycleclaims.com 24/7 365 days a year.

 

After I file a claim, when will I receive my replacement?

 

Your repaired device will be shipped back to you as soon as the repair is complete.

If we are unable to repair your product and it needs to be replaced, you may get one of the following, at our discretion:

A new, refurbished, or remanufactured product, or a product with like, kind, quality and functionality that performs to the factory specifications of the original product will be sent to you upon receipt of your broken device.

In some cases where we cannot provide an adequate replacement a reimbursement will be issued for the replacement value of the product. 

 

 

Do I need proof of purchase?

 

You will provide a date of purchase when you register your device but no proof of purchase is necessary.
